GAINING ALTITUDE

A BUSY TWO MONTHS

The first two months of 2012 have been a blur for the Rock ChMS project.  Much work has been accomplished (just check GitHub), and an exciting new partnership has been formed.  All of it is summarized below.

NewSpring Church Joins the Rock ChMS Team

The Spark Development Network is pleased to share that NewSpring Church is becoming a development partner on the Rock ChMS project.  Their involvement will increase the speed at which Rock is developed and will also bring new and innovative ideas to the project.  For NewSpring, Rock ChMS represents a next generation platform to take their church to the next level.

NewSpring’s involvement also highlights the collaborative nature that the Spark Development Network was created to foster: an eco-system where ideas from several churches can be forged into software that is freely available.

While there is still much road to travel, the addition of NewSpring to the team speeds the time to the finish line.
