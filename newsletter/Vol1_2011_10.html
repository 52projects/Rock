Newsletter Volume 1

OPEN

The Spark Development Network is Open for Business

This is our first communication since our announcement last week.  We’re surprised and excited by all of the interest and support we’ve received.  In an effort to be concise here are a couple of things to let you know about.

Rock ChMS  Update 

The Rock ChMS is the premiere project for the Spark Development Network. We’re still very much in the initial stage of its development with a long road ahead of us.  We hope to have an initial release sometime in late 2012.  If you’re an experienced .Net programmer we’d love some help.  

Spark Development Network Non-profit

The paperwork for creating the Spark Dev Network is proceeding quickly.  We’re now an official Arizona corporation and are working on getting our non-profit status.

How To Keep Up With Our Progress

There’s several ways you can keep in the loop on our progress.  The first is this newsletter.  We’ll try to send out a newsletter once a month.  If you want more details there’s several other channels for you to stay in touch.
 
Spark Facebook Page / Twitter Feed: Keep up to date with organizational details and progress related to the formation of the non-profit.
 
Rock ChMS Facebook Page / Twitter Feed: Keep a pulse on our development progress and thoughts on future features.
 
Code Check-ins:  For the true geeks you can get a summary of each code check-in by watching our GitHub feed.

Your Prayers Are Needed

Most of all we’d appreciate your prayer for this project.  While the team has written one successful ChMS solution we believe that past success isn’t a guarantee for future success, that we need God’s wisdom and guidance now just as we did in the past.  Please keep us in your prayers.

Questions?

If you have any questions please don’t hesitate to send them our way.  There’s probably several things we’ve forgot to communicate.  Send your questions to jon@sparkdevnetwork.org.
