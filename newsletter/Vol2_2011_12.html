ACCELERATION

Much Has Been Accomplished

Wheels are up, the Spark Development Network has taken off.  So much has been accomplished in the last month it will be hard to summarize it, but we've done our best below.

New Spark Website 

We released an enhanced Spark website that we feel better communicates our message.  This also gave us the opportunity to play with two new HTML/CSS frameworks (Twitter Bootstrap and ZURB's Framework).  The outcome of this research will be a well structured and very powerful HTML/CSS framework built into the Rock ChMS.  This will allow developers and designers alike to create consistently beautiful user interfaces in a fraction of the time.  Talking about killing two birds with one stone...

New Board Members
The Spark Development Network is up to its full five board members.  Jeremy Hoff (Shepherd of the Hills) and Nick Airdo (Central Christian Az) have joined David Turner, Tony Burgarello and Jon Edmiston to complete the board.  

We're Organized 

The Spark Development network is now fully incorporated in the State of Arizona and has completed its first Board of Directors Meeting.  We're actively completing the paperwork for our 501c3 status with the help of a local CPA.  Most importantly we now have an official bank account.  The only problem... it's empty.  If you'd like to donate a few dollars to help pay for our hosting fees (currently being paid from our personal resources) we'd be very appreciative (and so would our wives).  No gift too small.

Rock Progress

Our main focus on Rock has been to build a well crafted CMS to use as a foundation.  Work is moving well on this and is nearly complete.  We have some incredible ideas built into the CMS that will help us meet our goal of not just building an incrementally better ChMS but an exponentially better one.  Last week we had a two day meeting with Nick, David and Jon to review each line of code and define remaining action items.  We also painted a broad brush of ideas for features like workflow, groups and person management.  We can't wait to share them and to get your feedback!

Keep your eye on GitHub for the latest check-ins and dialog on the progress. Be sure to click the "Watch" button to have the latest updates emailed to you as they occur.

Questions?

If you have any questions please don’t hesitate to send them our way.  There’s probably several things we’ve forgot to communicate.  Send your questions to jon@sparkdevnetwork.org.
